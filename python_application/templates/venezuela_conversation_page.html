Overview: On this page, you can ask Señor Lex a question in Spanish. Señor Lex is a chatbot built with AWS Lex v2. Then, he will return a response in Spanish. This page's intention is for users to practice writing questions in Spanish and increase their written conversational ability. You may ask questions such as:

  • How are you?
  • What is your name?
  • Where are you from?
  • How old are you?
  • What do you like to do?
  • What music do you listen to?

and many more. In terms of grammar, it is advised to utilize an upside down question mark, ¿, at the start of your question. However, if the ¿ is omitted, Lex will still be able to interpret your question (provided the question is stated logically). Each question that Lex answers will go to your dictionary for your records.
